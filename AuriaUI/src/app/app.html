Power your day, the sustainable way!

How can current solar backpacks be improved to provide more reliable eco-friendly charging for students, and communities with limited power access?

About

Reliable access to energy remains a persistent challenge for students and communities that lack consistent electrical infrastructure. Therefore we are designing a solar backpack that can optimize energy capture, regulate heat, and better align with user needs.

Features

  • Solar Panel (5–10W flexible)
  • Rechargeable Battery Pack (10,000 mAh)
  • USB & Wireless Charging Module
  • Waterproof & Scratch-resistant Fabric
  • Inner Lining / Insulation Layer
  • Water Filter Module (portable mini filter)
  • Step Tracker Sensor (accelerometer)
  • GPS Module
  • Zippers & Fasteners
  • Thread, Webbing, Padding
  • Misc Electronics & Wiring

Overveiw

Through empathy and observation, we recognize how important power access is to mobility and independence, especially for people who travel, live remotely, or work in disaster-prone areas. The urgency of sustainability and the over-dependence on fossil-fueled energy sources further amplify this project's relevance. By addressing these issues through user-centered design, this project becomes more than academic; it becomes practical, timely, and socially valuable.
